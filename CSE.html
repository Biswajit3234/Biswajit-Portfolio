Computer Science Engineering

Widely appreciated as a royal department of GITA , the department of computer science & Engineering was established in 2004 with an initial intake of 60 seats and subsequently increased to 120 seats in 2009. The department strives to imbibe cutting-edge skills through research and development, industrial consultancy and high quality teaching.

  • The department is powered by 31 highly experienced faculty members with academic and research background and supported by 10 high skilled programmers and service engineers.
  • There are 10 state of the art laboratories to make the students technically sound in front areas like programming in ‘C’, Data structure, Database Engineering, Software Engineering, Computer Architecture, Java Programming etc.
  • The department has collaboration with different professional bodies and industrial houses to conduct workshop, seminar, short term courses etc.
  • The department facilitates the placement of deserving students in reputed industries like INFOSYS, TCS, TECH-MAHINDRA, CAPGEMINI, COGNIZANT , MINDTREE etc.
  • Today employees of many reputed industries and research scholars of premiere institutes are alumni of this department.


C Programming Lab

The Laboratory is an interactive environment for creating and conducting Programming in C experiments:
a playground for experimentation. It focuses more on the mechanics of writing a C program. The objective
of this lab is to make students familiar with different programs, their application and their use.
To understand how various Programming techniques. To understand some important applications of Programming
Language. To understand how the choice of Programming can lead to efficient implementations of algorithms.
The laboratory facilities available in the department are as under:
  • C Programming Lab
  • Data Structure Lab
  • Object Oriented Programming Lab (C++)
  • Database Engineering Lab
  • DAA Lab
  • Computer Networking Lab
  • Computer Organization Lab
  • Operating System Lab
  • Java Lab
  • Software Engineering Lab
